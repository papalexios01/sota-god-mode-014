## üö¶ Troubleshooting

### Issue: Content Lost After Transmutation

**Cause**: Anti-hallucination check too strict (60% threshold)
**Solution**: Reduce threshold to 0.5 (50%) in services.tsx line 1213

### Issue: Too Aggressive Entity Replacement

**Cause**: AI replacing correct entities with incorrect ones
**Solution**: Add protected terms to prompt or reduce batch size

### Issue: Transmutation Taking Too Long

**Cause**: Processing all 40 nodes with large batches
**Solution**: Reduce to 25 nodes or increase batch size to 10

### Issue: API Rate Limits

**Cause**: Too many transmutation calls
**Solution**: Increase delay between batches (line 1246) from 500ms to 1000ms
